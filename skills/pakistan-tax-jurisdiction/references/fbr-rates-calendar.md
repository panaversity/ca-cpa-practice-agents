## Minimum Tax (Section 113)

When normal tax liability is less than the minimum tax on turnover:

- Standard rate: 1.25% of turnover
- Applies regardless of whether the entity has taxable income
- Excess minimum tax paid is adjustable against future years' tax liability (carry forward)

## Key SRO Categories

SROs commonly modify:

- WHT rates for specific industries (IT, textiles, construction)
- Exemptions for specific zones (SEZs, export processing zones)
- Procedural requirements for filing and documentation
- Concessionary rates for specific transaction types

Always check the FBR SRO database for the current tax year before applying standard rates.
