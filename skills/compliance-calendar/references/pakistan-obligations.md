| Authority                              | Abbreviation | Jurisdiction                                         | Primary Legislation                                |
| -------------------------------------- | ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Federal Board of Revenue               | FBR          | Federal tax (income tax, federal sales tax, customs) | Income Tax Ordinance 2001, Sales Tax Act 1990      |
| Securities and Exchange Commission     | SECP         | Corporate regulation, securities, insurance          | Companies Act 2017, Securities Act 2015            |
| State Bank of Pakistan                 | SBP          | Banking regulation, foreign exchange                 | SBP Act 1956, Foreign Exchange Regulation Act 1947 |
| Pakistan Stock Exchange                | PSX          | Listed company requirements                          | PSX Regulations                                    |
| Punjab Revenue Authority               | PRA          | Punjab provincial sales tax on services              | Punjab Sales Tax on Services Act 2012              |
| Sindh Revenue Board                    | SRB          | Sindh provincial sales tax on services               | Sindh Sales Tax on Services Act 2011               |
| Employees Old-Age Benefits Institution | EOBI         | Employee social security                             | EOBI Act 1976                                      |
| Provincial Social Security             | PESSI/SESSI  | Employee welfare and health                          | Provincial social security ordinances              |

## Critical Deadlines Summary (Annual Cycle)

| Month     | Key Obligations                                                                                            |
| --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| July      | New tax year begins (Jul-Jun FY). Sales tax return for June due (18th).                                    |
| August    | Advance tax Q1 preparation. Sales tax return for July due (18th).                                          |
| September | Corporate and individual income tax returns due (30th). Advance tax Q1 due (15th).                         |
| October   | AGM deadline for listed companies (120 days from June 30). SECP annual return due (within 30 days of AGM). |
| November  | SECP financial statements filing (within 30 days of AGM).                                                  |
| December  | Advance tax Q2 due (15th). AGM deadline for private companies (180 days from June 30).                     |
| January   | Half-year reviewed accounts due for listed companies (30 days from Dec 31).                                |
| February  | -                                                                                                          |
| March     | Advance tax Q3 due (15th).                                                                                 |
| April     | Third quarter accounts due for listed companies (30 days from Mar 31).                                     |
| May       | -                                                                                                          |
| June      | Advance tax Q4 due (15th). Tax year end (30th).                                                            |

## Common Compliance Failures

Based on practitioner experience, the most frequently missed obligations:

1. Monthly WHT statements — often filed late due to payroll processing delays
2. Provincial sales tax returns — overlooked when entity operates across provinces
3. EOBI contributions — non-compliance discovered during audits
4. SECP Form 29 (change of directors) — 15-day deadline frequently missed
5. Beneficial ownership declarations (Form 45) — new requirement, low awareness
