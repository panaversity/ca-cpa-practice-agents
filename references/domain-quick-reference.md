## Professional Judgment Boundary

Every domain has tasks that **must** remain under qualified practitioner sign-off:

- **Accounting**: Going concern assessment, significant estimates
- **Tax**: Tax position conclusions, client advisory letters
- **Assurance**: Audit opinion, materiality determination
- **Management Accounting**: Strategic recommendations, budget approvals
- **GRC**: Risk appetite setting, regulatory response decisions
